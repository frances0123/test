WHAT IS CHIIKAWA

CHARACTERS

Chiikawa, Hachiware and Usagi

"Chiikawa" is an abbreviation of "some kind of small and cute creature" (nanka chiisakute kawaii yatsu) in Japanese. It is a story about small and cute characters with eccentric personalities who live in a fun yet dismal world. Chiikawa is created by the illustrator Nagano and uploaded as a webcomic to Twitter, published as a manga, and adapted into an anime.

Best friends


CHIIKAWA: It is a bit of a cry baby, but it is kind. It makes a living weeding and hunting.
HACHIWARE: Chiikawa's friend. It is cheerful and positive. It lives in a cave.
USAGI: Chiikawa's friend. It often loudly scream things like "ura" and "yaha".
MOMONGA: It is always acting cutely. It asks for all sorts of things.
KURIMANJU: It looks cute, but...? It is a gourmet who is very caring.
RAKKO: It is strong and skilled at battle. Chiikawa and their friends admire it.
